GoPro Quik Vid Contest



Win a GoPro HERO10 Camera by making and sharing the Coolest Edit with the GoPro Quik Vid Contest. How to participate:

  • Create a GoPro account and get 3 months of Quik free at: https://gopro.com/quik3free
  • Download the Quik App and log into your GoPro account: https://gopro.com/quik
  • Make Edits using video and photos from your Phone, and GoPro if you have one.
  • Be creative, try different GoPro Original songs with different themes, mix it up!
  • Share your best Edits on Instagram with hashtag #mauikiteandwingfest
  • Share as many edits as you like and anyone can join!
Winners will be chosen and announced during the awards ceremony.
